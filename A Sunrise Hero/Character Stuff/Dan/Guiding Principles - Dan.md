**PREMISE:** Dan, the right-hand man to the city's crime boss is falsely accused of treachery and loses everything. He bands together a ragtag group to combat the city's fire problem and regain his position of power.

**BLURB:** All Dan ever wanted was to enjoy his steamed dumplings in peace, but the city of Meroun just wouldn’t let him. Recently stripped of power and wealth, he desperately schemes his way back into his adoptive father’s good graces. He prides himself on being a good man in a world of cutthroats, but when one good deed requires a dozen loathsome sins, he’ll soon find out that there’s hardly any difference between a hero and a bastard.

**DESIGNING PRINCIPLE:**  The story of a man refusing to turn evil while holding on to power in a cutthroat environment.

**DAN'S ARC:** Delusion > Awakening > Responsibility

**Ghost:** As an orphan, he equates his worth to his adoptive father’s approval.

**Lie:** He should remain a servant of the Cardinal to show his gratitude.

**Truth:** He is a capable and sympathetic leader and should embrace leadership.

**Want:** To get back in the Cardinal's good graces and make him nobility.

**Need:** To act on his ambition and take ownership of his choices.

**THEME:** Should you forsake your own morality to seize power from those who would abuse it?

**Sub-Themes:** Living vicariously through others, empathy

**SHORT SYNOPSIS:** Dan, the right-hand man of the city's cartel boss, is accused of betrayal and stripped of power. He uses his wits and cunning to stop the summer fires and redeem himself while still retaining his principles. In the end he learns to take ownership of his life and what it truly means to lead.
