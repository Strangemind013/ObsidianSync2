**BLURB:** 

**DESIGNING PRINCIPLE:**  The story of how a woman has to learn to live for herself despite familial obligations.

**Truth:** She has to live her life for herself.

**Want:** To finish the mission and collect her pay.

**Need:** To do something she's passionate about.
