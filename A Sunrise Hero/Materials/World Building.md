
**1. King** - absolute ruler


**2. State Council** - Ten Members. Advises the king, Tutors his Heir. Members are comprised of officials from the Three Departments and Six Houses, the State Academy, 


**3. Six Houses**

1. House of Order - Law Enforcement, Punishments, Prisons

2. House of Steel - Military

3. House of Growth - Taxation, Finances, Census, Agriculture and Land

4. House of Scrolls - Diplomacy, Culture, Rites

5. House of Banners - Civil Appointments

6. House of Works - Public Works, Industry, Mining, Roads, Bridges, etc.


**4. Three Departments**

1. Department of State Affairs - Controlled the Six Houses,

2. Department of Decisions - Proposed and Wrote the Law 

3. Department of Lessons - Reviewed Law and Inspected Corruption
